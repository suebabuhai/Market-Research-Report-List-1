Insulating glass offers energy efficiency by reducing heat transfer, enhancing thermal comfort. Tempered glass is heat-treated for increased strength and safety, making it ideal for high-impact applications. Low-e glass features a special coating to reflect infrared energy, improving thermal performance while allowing visible light to pass through.
