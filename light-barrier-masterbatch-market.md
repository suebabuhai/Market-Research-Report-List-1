<p><p>The Light Barrier Masterbatch market is categorized into two primary types: UV and IR. UV masterbatches are designed to block ultraviolet light, protecting materials from degradation and extending their lifespan, commonly used in outdoor applications. On the other hand, IR masterbatches are formulated to reflect infrared radiation, enhancing thermal management and energy efficiency. Together, these masterbatches are essential in various industries, including packaging, automotive, and construction, to improve product durability and performance against light-related damage.</p></p>
