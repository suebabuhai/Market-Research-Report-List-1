<p><ul><li>All Glass Vacuum Tube</li><li>Glass-metal Vacuum Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tubular solar collector market is primarily divided into two types: all-glass vacuum tubes and glass-metal vacuum tubes. All-glass vacuum tubes consist of an inner and outer glass layer, providing superior thermal insulation and resistance to environmental conditions. In contrast, glass-metal vacuum tubes feature a metal outer layer providing added structural integrity but may have lower efficiency compared to all-glass designs. Both types are utilized in residential and commercial applications, focusing on harnessing solar energy for heating purposes.</p></p>
