<p><strong>The Overhead Line Fault Indicator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pole-mounted Indicator</li><li>Conductor-mounted Indicator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Overhead Line Fault Indicator market is divided into two primary types: pole-mounted indicators and conductor-mounted indicators. Pole-mounted indicators are installed on utility poles to monitor overhead power lines for faults, providing visible alerts for maintenance teams. In contrast, conductor-mounted indicators attach directly to the conductors themselves, allowing for immediate detection of faults along the line. Both types enhance the reliability of power distribution, minimize downtime, and improve response times during outages.</p></p>
