<p><p>The nondestructive testing (NDT) equipment market encompasses various techniques used to evaluate materials and components without causing damage. Key types include ultrasonic testing, which uses high-frequency sound waves; radiography testing, utilizing X-rays for imaging; and visual inspection testing for direct observation. Magnetic particle and electromagnetic testing detect surface and subsurface flaws in conductive materials. Eddy-current testing assesses conductivity and material thickness, while liquid penetrant testing reveals surface defects. Other methods include thermography, acoustic emission, terahertz imaging, and infrared testing, enhancing material evaluation.</p></p>
