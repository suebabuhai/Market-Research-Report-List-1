<p><p>The industrial grade soda ash market comprises two primary types: dense soda ash and light soda ash. Dense soda ash is characterized by its high bulk density, making it ideal for applications requiring compact packaging and efficient transportation, such as glass manufacturing. Light soda ash, on the other hand, has a lower bulk density and is commonly used in chemical production, water treatment, and detergents, where its solubility and reactivity are beneficial. Both types serve critical roles across various industries.</p></p>
