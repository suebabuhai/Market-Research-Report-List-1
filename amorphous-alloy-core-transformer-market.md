<p><p>The amorphous alloy core transformer market is divided into two primary types: oil-immersed and dry-type transformers. Oil-immersed transformers utilize a liquid insulating medium, offering better cooling and allowing higher power ratings, making them suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, dry-type transformers use air for insulation, providing environmental safety, lower maintenance, and reduced fire risk, making them ideal for indoor installations and urban settings. Both types benefit from amorphous alloys, enhancing energy efficiency and reducing losses.</p></p>
