<p><p>The servo motors market is primarily divided into two types: D.C. servo motors and A.C. servo motors. D.C. servo motors are known for their precise control, quick response, and ease of speed adjustment, making them ideal for low-torque applications. In contrast, A.C. servo motors offer higher efficiency, better thermal performance, and are suitable for high-speed, high-torque applications, often used in industrial automation. Both types are essential in robotics, CNC machinery, and other applications requiring accurate motion control.</p></p>
