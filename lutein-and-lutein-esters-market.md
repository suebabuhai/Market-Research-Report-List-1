<p><p>The lutein and lutein esters market can be segmented into two main types: free lutein and lutein esters. Free lutein is a bioactive form of carotenoid that is used in dietary supplements and functional foods for its antioxidant properties and eye health benefits. Both types serve distinct applications in nutrition, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
