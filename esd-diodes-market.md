<p><p>The ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) diodes market comprises various components designed to protect electronic circuits. Zener diodes regulate voltage and provide stable references. Varistors protect against voltage surges by changing resistance with applied voltage. Silicon Controlled Rectifiers (SCRs) manage high power and regulate current. Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) diodes safeguard against transient voltage spikes. Other ESD protection devices include diodes, capacitors, and resistors that enhance circuit reliability. Together, these components address different aspects of voltage and current protection in electronics.</p></p>
