<p><p>The skateboard wheels market primarily consists of two types: PVC (polyvinyl chloride) and PU (polyurethane). PVC wheels are generally less expensive, providing a durable option suitable for casual riding. However, they tend to offer less grip and cushioning. In contrast, PU wheels are favored for their superior performance, offering excellent grip, shock absorption, and a smoother ride, making them ideal for tricks and more intense riding. This distinction influences consumer choices based on performance needs and budget constraints.</p></p>
