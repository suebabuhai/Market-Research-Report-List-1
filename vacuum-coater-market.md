<p><p>The vacuum coater market comprises several types, each serving unique applications. Vacuum sputtering coating machines employ a plasma process to create durable coatings, suitable for semiconductor and solar panel production. Chemical vapor deposition (CVD) coating machines use chemical reactions to produce high-quality films for advanced materials.
