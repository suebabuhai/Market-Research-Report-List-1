<p><p>Nasal delivery devices are categorized into three main types: unit-dose, bi-dose, and multi-dose. Unit-dose devices provide a single application per use, ensuring precise dosing and reducing contamination risk. Bi-dose devices allow for two applications from a single container, offering convenience while maintaining efficacy. Multi-dose devices can deliver several doses through a single container, making them suitable for chronic conditions where ongoing treatment is needed. These classifications cater to various therapeutic needs and patient preferences in nasal drug delivery.</p></p>
