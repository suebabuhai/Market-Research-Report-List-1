<p><p>Row units are essential components in agricultural planting equipment, categorized primarily into mechanical and pneumatic market types. Mechanical row units utilize mechanical drive systems, relying on gears and chains to ensure precise planting depth and spacing. In contrast, pneumatic row units use air pressure to control seed placement, offering enhanced planting accuracy and the ability to handle various seed sizes. Both types are designed to improve crop yields and efficiency, catering to different farming needs and technologies in modern agriculture.</p></p>
