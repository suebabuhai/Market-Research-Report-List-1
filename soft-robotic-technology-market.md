<p><p>The soft robotic technology market comprises three primary types: soft robot grippers, inflatable robots, and exoskeleton robots. Soft robot grippers are designed to handle delicate objects with precision, ideal for industries like food and electronics. Inflatable robots utilize flexible structures that expand and contract, making them suitable for applications in medical devices and search-and-rescue operations. Exoskeleton robots provide support and enhance human strength, primarily used in rehabilitation and assistance for mobility-impaired individuals. Each type leverages soft materials for enhanced adaptability and safety.</p></p>
