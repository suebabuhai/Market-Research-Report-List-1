<p><p>The office paper shredder market is primarily categorized into three types: strip-cut, micro-cut, and cross-cut shredders. Strip-cut shredders slice paper into long strips, offering basic security but are less secure than other types. Cross-cut shredders provide enhanced security by cutting paper into smaller, confetti-like pieces. Micro-cut shredders provide the highest level of security, turning paper into tiny particles, making it virtually impossible to reconstruct. Each type caters to different security needs and office environments.</p></p>
