<p><strong>The Multifocal Intraocular Lens IOLs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Not Pre-loaded IOLs</li><li>Pre-loaded IOLs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multifocal intraocular lens (IOL) market is divided into two main types: non-preloaded IOLs and pre-loaded IOLs. Non-preloaded IOLs require manual loading into the surgical device by the surgeon before implantation, offering flexibility but demanding technical skill. In contrast, pre-loaded IOLs come pre-packaged in a delivery system, simplifying the surgical process and reducing the risk of handling errors. This differentiation caters to varying surgeon preferences and operational efficiencies in cataract surgery procedures.</p></p>
