<p><ul><li>Convection Ovens</li><li>Vapour Phase Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The post-reflow machine market primarily features Convection Ovens and Vapour Phase Ovens, both essential for PCB assembly processes. Convection Ovens utilize forced hot air to evenly distribute heat, ensuring uniform soldering for complex assemblies. They are widely favored for their efficiency and versatility. Vapour Phase Ovens, on the other hand, use vaporized solvents to transfer heat, providing precise temperature control and reducing thermal shock to sensitive components. Both types enhance reliability and performance in electronic manufacturing post-reflow.</p></p>
