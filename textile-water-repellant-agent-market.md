<p><p>The textile water repellent agent market is categorized into C8, C6, and C0 water repellent agents. C8 agents, based on perfluorooctanoic acid, provide superior water and stain resistance but face environmental scrutiny. C6 agents, derived from shorter-chain fluorocarbons, offer similar properties with a reduced environmental impact and are increasingly preferred. C0 agents use non-fluorinated technologies, providing eco-friendly alternatives for water repellency without compromising performance. Each type caters to varying consumer preferences and regulatory demands, shaping the market landscape.</p></p>
