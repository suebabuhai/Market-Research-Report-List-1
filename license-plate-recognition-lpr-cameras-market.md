<p><p>License Plate Recognition (LPR) cameras are categorized into three main types: mobile, fixed, and portable. Mobile LPR cameras are typically mounted on vehicles, enabling them to capture license plate data while in motion. Fixed LPR cameras are installed in stationary locations, such as entry points or toll booths, providing continuous monitoring. Portable LPR cameras offer flexibility, allowing users to move them easily for temporary installations or inspections. Each type serves distinct applications across security, traffic management, and law enforcement.</p></p>
