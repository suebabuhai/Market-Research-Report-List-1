<p><ul><li>Chemical vapor deposition</li><li>Liquid impregnation process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>C C Composite market types can be categorized primarily into two processes: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Liquid Impregnation. CVD involves depositing thin films of materials onto substrates through vapor-phase chemical reactions, resulting in high-performance coatings suitable for various applications. Liquid impregnation, on the other hand, refers to the process of infusing a liquid resin into a porous matrix, enhancing material properties like strength and durability. Both methods are integral in manufacturing advanced composites for industries such as aerospace and automotive.</p></p>
