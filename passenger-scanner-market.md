<p><p>The passenger scanner market comprises two primary types: X-ray passenger scanners and millimeter wave passenger scanners. X-ray scanners utilize radiation to generate detailed images of the contents within luggage and body, enhancing security by identifying concealed items. Millimeter wave scanners, on the other hand, use non-ionizing radio waves to create 3D images of individuals, detecting threats without revealing personal details. Both technologies are crucial in airports and secure facilities, ensuring passenger safety while facilitating efficient screening processes.</p></p>
