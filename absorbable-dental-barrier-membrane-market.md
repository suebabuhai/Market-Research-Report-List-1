Pigskin membranes, derived from porcine sources, offer flexibility and similar regenerative properties. Both types serve as effective barriers in dental surgeries, promoting optimal healing by preventing the infiltration of unwanted tissues while facilitating proper bone and soft tissue regeneration.</p></p>
