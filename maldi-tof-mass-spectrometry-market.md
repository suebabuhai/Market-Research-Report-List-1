<p><p>MALDI-TOF Mass Spectrometry (Matrix-Assisted Laser Desorption/Ionization Time-of-Flight Mass Spectrometry) is a powerful analytical technique used primarily for identifying and characterizing biomolecules, proteins, and other large organic compounds. The method utilizes a laser to ionize samples that are mixed with a matrix material, allowing for precise mass determination. This approach is widely employed in various fields, including proteomics, microbiology, and clinical diagnostics due to its speed, sensitivity, and ability to analyze complex mixtures.</p><p>The MALDI-TOF Mass Spectrometry Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for rapid diagnostic tools in healthcare and advancements in research applications.
