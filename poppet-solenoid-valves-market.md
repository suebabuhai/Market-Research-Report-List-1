<p><p>Poppet solenoid valves are crucial in fluid control systems, classified primarily into normally-closed and normally-open types. Normally-closed valves remain shut when de-energized, allowing flow only when the solenoid is activated, making them ideal for safety applications. Conversely, normally-open valves permit flow in their de-energized state, closing only upon activation, suitable for applications requiring continuous flow regulation. The choice between these valve types depends on specific operational requirements, including safety, flow control, and system design in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
