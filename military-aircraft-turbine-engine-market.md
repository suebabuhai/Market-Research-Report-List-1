Turbojet engines, known for their simplicity and high-speed capabilities, excel in high-performance aircraft, often used in older models. In contrast, turbofan engines offer greater fuel efficiency and reduced noise, making them the preferred choice for modern military aircraft. They operate at lower speeds and are more suitable for a range of missions, including transport and tactical operations. This diversity supports various military aviation needs.</p></p>
