<p><p>The polyester medical films market can be categorized into three primary types based on thickness: Under 100 μm, 100-300 μm, and Above 300 μm. Films under 100 μm are typically used for applications requiring flexibility and breathability, such as wound dressings and transdermal patches. The 100-300 μm category offers enhanced strength and durability, suitable for surgical drapes and medical packaging. Finally, films above 300 μm provide robust protection and are ideal for equipment covers and protective barriers in healthcare settings.</p></p>
