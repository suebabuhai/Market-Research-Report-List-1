<p><strong>The Bathroom Ventilation Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ceiling Fan</li><li>Wall-Mounted Fan</li><li>Window-Mounted Fan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bathroom ventilation fan market consists of three main types: ceiling fans, wall-mounted fans, and window-mounted fans. Ceiling fans are installed in the roof space, offering efficient air circulation and moisture removal. Wall-mounted fans are attached to the wall, ideal for smaller bathrooms, providing direct ventilation. Window-mounted fans are designed to fit in window frames, allowing for both exhaust and intake of air, promoting cross-ventilation. Each type aids in moisture control, preventing mold growth and improving air quality.</p></p>
