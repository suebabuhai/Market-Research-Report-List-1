<p><strong>The Compression Bonded Magnets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bonded NdFeB Magnets</li><li>Bonded SmCo Magnets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Compression Bonded Magnets market includes two primary types: Bonded NdFeB Magnets and Bonded SmCo Magnets. Bonded NdFeB Magnets, made from neodymium-iron-boron, offer high magnetic strength and are suitable for a variety of applications, including automotive and consumer electronics. In contrast, Bonded SmCo Magnets, composed of samarium-cobalt, provide superior thermal stability and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for high-temperature environments. Both types benefit from a bonding process that enhances their mechanical properties and allows for complex shapes.</p></p>
