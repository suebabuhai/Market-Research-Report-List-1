<p><strong>The CMP Pad Regulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Pad Conditioners</li><li>CVD Diamond Pad Conditioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMP pad regulator market includes two primary types: conventional pad conditioners and CVD diamond pad conditioners. Conventional pad conditioners utilize various abrasives to maintain the flatness and performance of chemical mechanical polishing (CMP) pads, enhancing their lifespan. In contrast, CVD diamond pad conditioners leverage chemical vapor deposition technology to produce diamond-coated surfaces, offering superior durability and efficiency. Both types aim to optimize the polishing process in semiconductor and electronic device manufacturing, catering to different needs in terms of performance and cost.</p></p>
