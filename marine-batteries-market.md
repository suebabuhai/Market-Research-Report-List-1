<p><strong>The Marine Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gel Battery</li><li>Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) Battery</li><li>Flooded Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine batteries market features three primary types: Gel Batteries, which use silica to immobilize electrolyte, offering resistance to vibration and spillage; Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) Batteries, which capture electrolyte in glass mats for superior leakage protection and maintenance-free operation; and Flooded Batteries, filled with liquid electrolyte, known for their cost-effectiveness and robust performance but requiring regular maintenance. Each type serves distinct marine applications, balancing factors like power capacity, weight, and environmental conditions for optimal resilience and efficiency.</p></p>
