<p><ul><li>Veneer Type</li><li>Segmented Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flat metal detectors are categorized mainly into veneer type and segmented type. Veneer type detectors are designed for thin materials, offering effective detection for flat items like wood veneers or sheets. They provide a uniform detection field, ensuring consistent performance across the surface. In contrast, segmented type detectors feature multiple detection zones, enabling targeted monitoring of specific areas. This allows for greater sensitivity and accuracy in identifying metal contaminants in a variety of materials, enhancing quality control in various industries.</p></p>
