<p><p>Adoptive Cell Therapy encompasses various market types, including CAR-T, TCR-T, NK, and TIL therapies. CAR-T (Chimeric Antigen Receptor T-cell) therapy modifies T-cells to target specific cancer antigens. TCR-T (T-cell Receptor Therapy) enhances T-cell recognition of tumor antigens. NK (Natural Killer) cell therapy employs innate immune cells to attack tumors without prior sensitization. TIL (Tumor-Infiltrating Lymphocyte) therapy extracts and expands T-cells from tumors, promoting an immune response against similar cancer cells, highlighting diverse approaches in cancer treatment.</p></p>
