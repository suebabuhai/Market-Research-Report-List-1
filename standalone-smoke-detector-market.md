<p><p>The Standalone Smoke Detector market features two primary types: Ionization and Photoelectric smoke detectors. Ionization smoke detectors are highly sensitive to fast-flaming fires, utilizing radioactive material to detect smoke particles. Meanwhile, Photoelectric smoke detectors employ a light beam and sensor to identify slow-smoldering fires, making them effective for detecting dense smoke. Both types serve crucial roles in fire safety, catering to different fire scenarios and providing essential early-warning features for residential and commercial properties.</p></p>
